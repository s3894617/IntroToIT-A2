Insights

All team members personality testing results portray them as introverts- logically, communication is likely to be more difficult and it may be the case that participation in team meetings may be limited until team members are more comfortable with each other or the team dynamic itself. Due to the presence of introverted personalities it is likely that a team member will need to take on the responsibility of encouraging participation in team meetings as well as attempting to drive regular communication regarding work progress. Interestingly, 3 members of the team (Ishaan, Nima and Sam) all identify as "Logisticians"- as a persoanlity type, Logisticians often tend to be more analytical thinkers and prefer to work alone- which may add further difficulty to team communication, if such traits prove to be accurate.
